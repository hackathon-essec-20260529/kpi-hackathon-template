ACME Corp — Annual Report 2023

Financial highlights

ACME Corp delivered a solid fiscal year 2023. Consolidated Group revenue
reached 3.8 billion euros, up from 3.5 billion euros in 2022. Adjusted
EBITDA for the Group stood at 450 million euros in 2023, broadly in line
with the prior year.

European operations contributed 1.4 billion euros of revenue in 2023
(versus 1.3 billion euros in 2022). North America accounted for the
remainder, weighted toward the second half of the year.

Headcount at the end of fiscal year 2023 reached 12,500 employees across
all geographies.
